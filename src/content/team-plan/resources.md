## Additional Resources

- [USA Hockey - A New Approach to Goaltender Development](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/news_article/show/1156979) - Article from USA Hockey outlining practice design and game management for youth goalies at different age levels.
- [USA Hockey Goaltending Homepage](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/) - Home for USA Hockey Goaltending.
- [USA Hockey Goaltender Basics](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/page/show/866192-goaltender-basics) - This is a great resource for coaches to learn the basic goaltending terms and techniques for stance, movement, and positioning to help instruct their team's goalies.
- [USA Hockey Goaltender Season Development Practice Plans](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/page/show/2872653-goaltending-practice-plans) - Drills and practice plans for coaches to help develop their team's goalies during each practice.
- [USA Hockey Goalie Plans](https://www.usahockey.com/goaltendingplans) - Official USA Hockey goaltending plans and coaching resources to help coaches develop structured practice plans and assessment tools for their goalies.
- [USA Hockey Goaltending Game Situation Drills](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/page/show/890215-game-situation-drills) - A collection of drills designed to help coaches teach their goalies how to handle various game situations effectively.
