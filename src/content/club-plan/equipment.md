## Equipment Requirements

All goalies will need the following equipment:

- skates (goalie skates recommended for goalies 10U+)
- leg pads
- knee pads (if not integrated into younger player leg pads)
- hockey pants (goalie pants recommended for goalies 12U+)
- cup (goalie cup recommended for 10U+)
- chest protector
- goalie glove
- goalie blocker
- goalie stick
- neck guard (goalie neck guard recommended)
- helmet (goalie helmet recommended, must be HECC certified)
- helmet "dangler" recommended
