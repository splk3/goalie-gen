## Additional Resources

- [How to Structure a Goaltending Development Program](https://worldhockeylab.com/how_to_structure_a_goaltending_development_program/): This is a great resource on how to structure a goaltending development program for your club. It's written by Hiroki Wakabayashi as part of his USA Hockey Goaltending Gold project in 2024.
- [Example Plan for Youth Club Goaltending Coach Development - South Windsor Youth Hockey Association](https://www.swhockey.com/coaching-resources/goaltending-coach-development/10190): This is a great example of a well-structured plan for developing goaltending coaches and implementing goalie-focused coaching for teams at the youth club level.
- [USA Hockey Goaltending Homepage](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/) - Home for USA Hockey Goaltending.
