## Additional Resources

- [How to Structure a Goaltending Development Program](https://worldhockeylab.com/how_to_structure_a_goaltending_development_program/): This is a great overview of how to structure a goaltending development program for your club. It's written by Hiroki Wakabayashi, who was part of the USA Hockey Goaltending Gold Class of 2024.
- [Example Plan for Youth Club Goaltending Coach Development - South Windsor Youth Hockey Association](https://www.swhockey.com/coaching-resources/goaltending-coach-development/10190): This is a great example of a well-structured plan for developing goaltending coaches at the youth club level.
