## Additional Resources

- [USA Hockey Goaltender Basics](https://www.usahockeygoaltending.com/page/show/866192-goaltender-basics) - This is a great resource to share with your parents parents to help them learn basic goaltending terms and techniques for stance, movement, and positioning.
- [How to Break in a Goalie Glove - StaubersGoalcrease](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1_-2dWL0KiA) - Need help breaking in a new glove? Younger goalies sometimes stuggle to get comfortable getting their glove worked in to the point where they can open and close it effectively. This video will help!
